## CRITICAL CORRECTIONS FROM LAST RENDER

The last version had these problems — fix ALL of them:

1. **NO 3-6-9 on the splash screen.** 369 is backend architecture only. Remove the clock/369 symbol entirely. Use a simple geometric mark: a thin white circle with a single small gold diamond at center. That's it.

2. **Typography must be EB Garamond** for the app name "ALCHEMIZE" and "the work of becoming" — serif, elegant, weight 600-700. Not bold sans-serif. Body/UI labels can be Inter (sans-serif).

3. **NO mystical nav labels.** No "Sanctum", "Atlas", "Journey". Use: "Today", "Journal", "Cycle", "Settings" or simple geometric icons with no text labels.

4. **Reduce gold by 80%.** Use champagne gold (#D4A54A) as a SINGLE hairline accent — one thin line, one small diamond. Primary palette: matte black (#000000), dark charcoal cards (#0D0D0D), white text (#FFFFFF). The gold should be barely there — an accent, not a theme.

5. **The 7 laws should NOT be a wheel/diagram.** Each session shows ONE law at the top as a simple bold label: "Correspondence · As above, so below" in Inter Bold — just text, no symbols, no wheel.

6. **No mystical imagery.** No crystals, no glowing auras, no spiritual symbols. Clean geometric forms only — circles, lines, diamonds.

7. **No "REVEAL" or "INTEGRATE" buttons.** Keep interactions clean: "Save" or just a subtle checkmark. No action names that sound ritualistic.

8. **Background is pure black #000000** — not dark charcoal, not gradient. Pure black void.
