Logo Explorations — Direction D: The Alchemical Vessel
D1 · Classic Hexagram
Upward triangle (gold) = reaching toward light
Downward triangle (white) = shadow descending
6 outer points + 3 intersections = 9
D2 · The Seed
Triangles don't touch — the center is held sacred
Gap = space between shadow and light
Seed = the self yet to be completed
D3 · Nested
One triangle inside another — not crossing
Outer gold = container of the work
Inner white = the shadow held within
D4 · Broken Threshold
One triangle dashed = the work is incomplete
One solid = the shadow is always there
Small circle at top = the goal visible but not reached
D5 · Three Rings
Both triangles in gold — unity of light and shadow
3 concentric rings at center = the 3 inputs
Outer = 6 points, inner = 3 rings, total = 9
D6 · The Empty Vessel
Thick container = the practice itself
Faint inner geometry = the hidden engine (astrology)
Gold arc at top + diamond at bottom = the work's span
The 3-6-9 in the geometry

3 — The triangles (two interlocking, or the three elements of the emblem)
6 — The outer points of the star, or the spaces between intersections
9 — The total: 6 points + 3 intersections, or 3 rings × 3 axes, or 3+6
